How it began

Our Love Story

✦ ✦ ✦
June 2019

First Meeting

They met by chance at a mutual friend's rooftop party in Manhattan. Sarah was admiring the city lights when James offered her his jacket. One conversation turned into hours.

🌇
December 2019

First Date

James surprised Sarah with a private dinner cruise around the harbor. The winter air was cold, but neither of them noticed — they were too wrapped up in each other's stories.

🚢
March 2021

Moving In Together

After surviving a pandemic together (mostly on video calls), they finally shared a home. The tiny apartment in Brooklyn felt like a palace because they were in it together.

🏠
September 2024

The Proposal

James proposed at the same rooftop where they first met, now strung with fairy lights and filled with their closest friends hidden in the shadows. Sarah said yes before he could finish the question.

💍
June 14, 2025

Forever Begins

And now, they invite you to witness the most beautiful chapter of their story yet.
